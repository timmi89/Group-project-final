About Us

Polyamory is, simply put, the capacity to love many. Our site brings together all kinds of people in a web community setting and creates a place for them to connect and kindle romance.

This is a safe space for those who identify as:
Poly • Polyamorous • Ethical Non-Monogamist • Ethical Hedonist • Ethical Swinger • Polyamorist • Prefer not to self identify but feel that monogamy is too limiting

Not only do we provide a tasteful adult environment; bringing people together for love, friendship, or whatever type or relationship you're seeking. We also seek to be a resource for learning about Polyamory in its many forms and nuances. The practice of Polyamory can be as unique as each of us are. We hope to help you find what you're looking for.

A Brief History of Polyamory

What exactly is "polyamory" and what is all the fuss about it?

The definition of and history of Polyamory: "Polyamory" is a recently coined word that was derived by combining the Greek word "poly" meaning many, and the Latin word "amor" meaning love. Polyamory is intended to mean many loves. The exact definition of polyamory is not so clear, but when the word is used, the intent is generally meant to imply the state in which an individual can love more than one person romantically and simultaneously
